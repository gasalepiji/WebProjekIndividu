HELL IN THE MIDS OF STORMS









In the year 3765, across the vast reaches of th e galaxy, five cosmic beings, Airy, Ei, Allen, Alfie, and Firefly who born from the infinite essence of the universe, find themselves entangled in a monumental war against the Fordials, enigmatic entities from the Xynolithium dimension. The Fordials are not mere physical foes, They are living voids, beings beyond physical comprehension and definition.


As the battle reaches its zenith, an unknown portal appears, pulling the five cosmic warriors into Earth. A planet entirely different from their world. Stranded in the era of World War III, they are thrust into a chaotic realm of primitive technology and intricate political schemes.


Amid the turmoil, a secret group known as the Thanamos Cult approaches them, pleading for help to end the war. However, the request is shrouded in hidden motives. Ei, Allen, and Firefly choose to align with the cult, hoping to restore peace. Meanwhile, Airy and Alfie refuse, suspecting a sinister agenda behind the offer.


Their defiance leads to catastrophe. Betrayed and captured, Airy and Alfie are imprisoned by magical shackles that torment their bodies and souls. Yet, they are no ordinary beings. The cosmic power within them begins to awaken, granting them hope for retaliation.


In a daring escape, Airy and Alfie fight to regain their freedom, uncover the dark plans of the Thanamos Cult, and save their allies from doom. But this struggle is more than just a bid for freedom, it is a battle to unravel the universe’s deepest secrets, restore balance, and determine the fate of a world teetering on the brink of destruction.


Will they succeed in saving Earth and uncovering the truth behind the forces that bind them? Or will they become trapped in a cycle of conflict that consumes everything?


Hell In The Mids Of Storms is a tale of action, mystery, and sacrifice that pushes the boundaries of power and trust. It’s an epic journey across dimensions, battling unseen enemies, and discovering the true meaning of the cosmic force they possess.
